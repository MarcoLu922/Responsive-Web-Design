Tribute to Dr. Norman Borlaug
Dr. Norman Borlaug, the Father of the Green Revolution
Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist who is considered one of the most influential figures in agricultural history. He is credited with developing high-yielding crop varieties and advancing modern agricultural techniques, which significantly contributed to feeding billions of people around the world. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 for his work in combating world hunger.
